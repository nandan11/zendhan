Large-Cap Equity Funds

Detailed explanation of large-cap funds...

Key Features of Large-Cap Funds

  • Invest in companies with market capitalization above ₹20,000 crore
  • Relatively stable compared to mid and small-cap funds
  • Suitable for conservative equity investors

Mid-Cap Equity Funds

Detailed explanation of Mid-cap funds...

Key Features of Mid-Cap Funds

  • Invest in companies with market capitalization between ₹1,000 and ₹20,000 crore
  • Slighly less stable compared to large cap but more stable than small-cap funds
  • Suitable for someone with a little more risk appetite

Small-Cap Equity Funds

Detailed explanation of Small-cap funds...

Key Features of Small-Cap Funds

  • Invest in companies with market capitalization less than ₹1,000 crore
  • High volatility and less stable than large and mid-cap funds
  • Suitable for someone with a high risk tolerance looking to maximize returns/li>
